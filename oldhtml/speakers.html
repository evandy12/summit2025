Summit Speakers

Our Distinguished Speakers

Learn from industry experts and thought leaders in supply chain management

Dr. John Smith

Professor of Supply Chain Management

University of Ghana Business School

Dr. Smith is a renowned expert in sustainable supply chain practices with over 15 years of experience in research and industry collaboration. His work focuses on developing resilient supply chain networks in Africa.

Prof. Sarah Johnson

Director of Supply Chain Innovation

African Development Institute

Professor Johnson leads groundbreaking research in digital transformation of supply chains. Her work has been instrumental in implementing smart logistics solutions across West Africa.

Dr. Michael Osei

Head of Logistics Operations

West African Trade Organization

With extensive experience in regional trade and logistics, Dr. Osei specializes in optimizing cross-border supply chain operations and implementing innovative solutions for complex logistics challenges in West Africa.

Dr. Aisha Mohammed

Supply Chain Sustainability Expert

African Center for Sustainable Development

Dr. Mohammed is a leading voice in sustainable supply chain practices across Africa. Her research focuses on integrating environmental consciousness with efficient supply chain management in developing economies.

Prof. David Kwame

Digital Supply Chain Strategist

Tech Innovation Hub, Lagos

Professor Kwame brings together technology and supply chain management, pioneering the implementation of blockchain and IoT solutions in African supply chains. His work has transformed how businesses approach digital transformation in logistics.
