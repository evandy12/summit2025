Lagos Travel Guide

Essential information for all summit participants traveling to Lagos

Your Summit Destination

As you prepare for the CARISCA Summit 2025, let this guide help you plan your stay in Africa's most dynamic city.

Welcome Summit Participants

This comprehensive guide is designed specifically for CARISCA Summit 2025 attendees to ensure a smooth and enjoyable stay in Lagos. Whether you're a first-time visitor to Nigeria or returning, you'll find essential information to help you prepare for the summit.

As you join us for this important gathering of supply chain professionals and researchers, we want to ensure you have all the information needed to navigate Lagos confidently. From arrival preparations to local transportation and accommodation, this guide covers everything summit participants need to know.

Our team has carefully curated this information to help you focus on what matters most - participating fully in the summit while experiencing the unique energy and hospitality of Lagos.

What to Expect During Your Stay

As a summit participant, you'll be primarily based in the Ikeja area, where our conference venue is located. Here's what you need to know about the key areas you might visit:

  • Ikeja (Summit Venue) - The commercial hub and capital of Lagos State
  • Victoria Island - Business district with recommended hotels and restaurants
  • Mainland - Where you'll find local markets and cultural sites
Summit Essentials
  • Time Zone: WAT (GMT+1) - Plan your arrival accordingly
  • Language: Summit is in English; local languages: Yoruba, Pidgin
  • Currency: Nigerian Naira (₦) - Exchange available at airport
  • Power: 230V, 50Hz (Type D & G) - Bring adapters for your devices

Weather Info

Weather in May
What to Expect
  • Average Temperature: 27-32°C (81-90°F)
  • Rainy Season: Occasional showers
  • Humidity: High (75-85%)
Preparation Tips
  • Pack a light raincoat or umbrella
  • Light, breathable clothing
  • Stay hydrated
  • Bring sunscreen (UV index is high)

Travel Requirements

Visa Requirements
  • Valid passport (6+ months)
  • Nigerian visa required
  • Apply well in advance
Health Requirements
  • Yellow Fever vaccination required
  • Malaria prevention recommended
  • Travel insurance advised

Transportation During Your Stay

Recommended Services

Uber and Bolt are preferred for summit participants. Available throughout Lagos.

Airport Transfer

Use shuttles provided by your hotel or labeled airport taxis for safe and reliable transport.

Local Navigation

Download offline maps before arrival. Summit app will include venue directions.

Safety Tips

  • Stay aware of your surroundings
  • Limit night travel
  • Keep conversations about money or plans private
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
  • Use recommended transportation options
  • Travel in groups when possible
  • Stick to well-known areas, especially at night
  • Keep basic medical supplies with you

Staying Connected

Mobile Networks
  • MTN
  • Airtel
  • Globacom
  • 9Mobile
Getting a SIM Card

Purchase at the airport or local stores. Bring your passport for registration.

Wi-Fi is available at the conference venue and most hotels.
